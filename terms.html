Before you start

These flashcards summarise the key terms of using the Trading Places prototype. They are written in plain language for testing purposes and are not a full legal agreement. In a production system, a formal Terms of Service document would also apply.

Card 1
How your data is used

This prototype records your choices in training scenarios to calculate an empathy score and to generate anonymised statistics. Individual answers are not shown to your line manager or peers. HR and programme owners may see only aggregated, de-identified results.

Card 2
Role-based access

Employees see only their own training progress and scores. Managers see anonymised insights for their team. HR sees organisation-wide trends, also anonymised. No role can access raw, named scenario choices for another individual through this prototype.

Card 3
Prototype status

Trading Places is currently a prototype. Content, scores, and interfaces may change without notice. Scenario results are intended for learning and reflection only and should not be used as formal performance ratings or disciplinary evidence.

Card 4
Respectful use

Scenarios may include sensitive themes such as bias, discrimination, and exclusion. By using this prototype you agree to engage respectfully and to follow your organisation’s code of conduct when discussing or sharing scenario content.

Card 5
Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data stored in this prototype environment by contacting the project owner or Data Protection Officer. In a live deployment, formal processes under GDPR and other applicable laws would apply.
